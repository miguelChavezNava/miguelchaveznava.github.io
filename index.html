About Me

I am a part of Emory University's class of 2027, pursuing a B.S in computer science and a B.A in film and media studies.

I have a fond interest in technology and media as I've started programming in middle school. I enjoy learning more about these fields and solving these different complex problems with unique and innovative solutions of algorithms and data structures.

I've also found an interest in film making, learning about the history of film and how a film is made. Making films have been fun to understand how to get certain story beats and emotions across in a creative way with a low budget.

Outside of academic interests, I love playing video games and watching new and old movies as it allows me to unwind and have some fun. I also love listening to music, specifically rock and hip-hop. These personal interests serve as inspiration for my achedemic pursuits.
